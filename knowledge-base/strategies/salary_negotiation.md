# Salary Negotiation & Anchoring Strategy

### The "Staff Architect" Premium
**Rule:** Always target the upper quartile of the published range. Stating a high-end number signals "Technical Authority."

### Response Templates:
*   **Tier 1 (SpotOn/Netflix):** "Targeting a base of $280,000+ to reflect my 20+ years of architectural maturity and specialized focus on multi-agent orchestration."
*   **Tier 2 (Webflow/Ubisoft):** "Targeting $225,000 base to reflect my ability to deliver 80x performance gains via AI-native architecture."
*   **The "AI Discount" Logic:** "My current baseline is $160k. I am negotiable down to the mid-$130s floor ONLY for roles offering exceptional, 100% billable growth in AI/ML Platform engineering."

### Why this works:
It establishes you as a "Unicorn" candidate (Master Architect + AI Implementer) who understands their market value.
